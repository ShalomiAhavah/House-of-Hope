What we do

The House of Hope Foundation is a non-profit organisation in Our aim is to facilitate cooperation between underpriviledged citizens, local contractors and sponsors, in order to enable each Namibian to become an owner of a solid home. The current pilot project is running in the village of Bethanie, in Namibia’s remote southwest, where we aim to build 100 houses by the end of 2030

How it Works

The House of Hope Foundation is a non-profit organisation which facilitates the replacement of shacks by solid brick structures, while promoting real and lasting ownership, dignity and hope.
The beauty of this principle is that the beneficiaries are fully-paid owners of the house from the laying of the foundation.
